You will be given a random (target) number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals above. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total so far.

You win the game by matching your total to the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
